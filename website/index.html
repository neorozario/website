How we craft it..

Imagine the iconic Krabby Patty transformed into a gourmet culinary masterpiece known as the Krusty Burger.

This elevated dish features a succulent, grass-fed beef patty, expertly grilled to perfection and seasoned with a blend of exotic spices.

The patty is nestled between two freshly baked, artisanal brioche buns, lightly toasted to achieve a delicate crunch.
